No matter what thoughts run through people's minds on this path, their footsteps always lead them down the most comfortable straight line. To break this familiar pattern, we presented forks in the road on the floor and allowed people to verify their choices through real-time results. Consequently, people willingly changed their habitual paths to carry out their choices, and just like the accumulated numbers you are seeing now, their actual footsteps came together to complete a new collection of movements in this space.
